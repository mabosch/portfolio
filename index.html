About me

Since I finished my architecture studies I’ve always been working in the design world. I love exploring all the ways how design can help us find solutions to almost everything, and make our lives as easy and fun as possible!

For some years I’ve worked in a little workshop in the middle of forest of Collserola (next to Barcelona), designing and producing wooden furniture. One day, while I was visiting a friend in California, I had the chance to discover the concept of UX design. I completely fell in love with it, and I decided to begin my path as a UX/UI designer.

I love working in challenging projects, and if possible, surrounded by nice people doing good teamwork. I believe the key to reach a good solution to any problem is understanding all the people involved and their point of view.

However, I must admit that my biggest passion is travelling around the world! I’ve had the luck to be in some amazing places such as Indonesia, Ecuador, Philippines or Tanzania where in 2015 I climbed the Mount Kilimanjaro. (I’m really proud of that! :D)
